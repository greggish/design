DC Open211

When someone in our community is in need of some kind of assistance, it’s hard to know what resources are available to them. It shouldn’t be. We can fix this.

Home

The DC Open211 Project is working to make it easier to produce, find, and use information about the community resources that are available to people in need throughout the greater Washington DC area.

We are working with governments, non-profit organizations, civic institutions, and community leaders to develop open platforms for the sharing of directory data about health, human, and social services -- in order to make this critical information accessible throughout an ecosystem of tools and applications, from call centers to case management systems to web engines like Yelp and Google.
